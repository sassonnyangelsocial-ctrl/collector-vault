Collector Vault gives collectors one place to organize their collection, wishlist, ISOs, DISOs and trades; follow restock and launch alerts; and use Seller Pro tools for orders, suppliers, sales and estimated profit.

Primary CTA: **Join Collector Vault — link in bio.**

Do not advertise guaranteed restocks, guaranteed market prices, guaranteed profits, or an official affiliation with Sonny Angel, Dreams Inc., Whatnot, Twilio or any retailer. Use “estimated market price” and “alerts from sources you follow.”

- Track: profile visits, link clicks, checkout starts, paid subscribers and monthly-vs-yearly mix.
- Record real app screens; generated graphics are campaign art, not exact product screenshots.
- Ask new members one onboarding question: “What do you want Collector Vault to help you do first?”
- After the first 10 users, use their answers to revise the homepage headline and next Reel hook.

Start with a small test, not a broad campaign: boost Reel 1 to adult collectible/toy audiences in the locations the service supports. Run the collector and seller messages as separate ad sets.

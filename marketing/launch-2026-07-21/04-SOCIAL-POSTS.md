## Trade Chat launch post

Found your match? Now start the conversation inside Collector Vault. Opt into Trade Chat, keep your ISO/DISO and ready-to-trade lists current, and the app surfaces reciprocal matches—what you want from them and what they want from you. Open a private conversation tied to the proposed trade, accept or close the discussion, and block or report when needed. Always verify condition and identity, use tracked shipping, and never share passwords or verification codes. Collector Vault does not guarantee users or trades. #SonnyAngelTrade #CollectorCommunity #TradeMatch #CollectorVault

Campaign graphics are conceptual promotional art, not screenshots of exact live data. Use real screen recordings whenever demonstrating product behavior.

## Posting specifications

- Feed graphics: upload the supplied square PNG at full quality.
- Reels/TikTok/Shorts: 1080×1920, 9:16, captions inside the central safe area.
- Stories: 1080×1920; keep link stickers above the bottom navigation area.
- Product Hunt: use the main launch graphic first, then real app screenshots of Dashboard, figure detail, Trade Chat, Alerts, Seller Pro overview, detailed order entry, Whatnot CSV import, and giveaway wheel.
- Email: link to a hosted asset or attach only the relevant compressed graphic; do not send all assets in a cold email.

## Required real screenshots

Capture these after signing into a test account containing sample—not customer—data:

1. Dashboard with collection progress.
2. Full-directory search and a figure detail panel.
3. Wishlist, ISO, DISO, and ready-to-trade states.
4. Reciprocal Trade Chat match and a sample conversation between two test accounts.
5. Alert categories, store watchlist, and SMS preferences—hide the phone number.
6. Seller Pro overview with sample values labeled as sample.
7. Detailed purchase-order form.
8. Supplier CRM.
9. Whatnot CSV import explanation—never show a real buyer's name/order.
10. Free-giveaway wheel setup and private draw history using fictional entrants.
11. Membership and phone-install screens.

## Visual disclosures

- Never place a Sonny Angel, Whatnot, eBay, retailer, or creator logo on a graphic without written permission.
- Do not use official figure photography as decorative campaign art without permission; product-source images may remain inside factual catalog views where appropriately sourced.
- Add `Independent app • Not affiliated with Sonny Angel, Dreams Inc., or Whatnot` to the final frame of product videos.
- Add `Estimates only` when price or profit fields appear.
- Add `Free entry only` whenever the wheel appears.
- Add `Verify before trading` whenever Trade Chat appears.

# Collector Vault media kit

## Product

Collector Vault is a subscription collection-management app for collectors and collectible sellers. The responsive web app can be installed directly to an iPhone or Android home screen.

## Live URL

https://collector-vault-one.vercel.app

## One sentence

Collector Vault replaces scattered screenshots, notes, and spreadsheets with one organized home for collections, wishlists, ISOs, DISOs, trades, alerts, and seller operations.

## Core features

- Collection, wishlist, ISO, DISO, and trade organization
- Catalog images displayed with full artwork visible
- Source-following restock, drop, launch, and inventory alerts
- Optional SMS alert preferences when messaging service is available
- Seller Pro order, supplier, sales-import, fee, cost, and estimated-profit tools
- Free giveaway-selection wheel with audit history
- Direct phone installation without an app-store account

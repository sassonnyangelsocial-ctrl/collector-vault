# Launch-day checklist

## Before promotion

- Test sign-up, email confirmation, login, monthly checkout, yearly checkout, and sign-out on a phone.
- Tap **Install app** before signing up and verify the instructions.
- Test the dashboard, collection, wishlist, ISO, DISO, trades, Trade Chat matching/messages, alerts, and Seller Pro.
- Confirm the Stripe success and cancellation paths return to the live site.
- Confirm SMS verification with one real phone number. If Twilio is still in trial mode, do not promise SMS access to every subscriber.
- Do not promote live market-price estimates until the approved eBay production credentials are installed and real results have been checked.
- Put this link in every profile: `https://collector-vault-one.vercel.app/?utm_source=profile&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=launch_2026_07_21`

## Launch sequence

### Hour 0

- Publish the launch post on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook Page, and Threads.
- Pin the Instagram post.
- Add the live link to every bio.
- Publish Story frames 1–3 from `04-SOCIAL-POSTS.md`.

### Hour 1

- Publish the 20-second collector Reel/TikTok.
- Reply to genuine comments; do not mass-DM commenters.
- Send personalized emails to only the first three retailer targets in the tracker.

### Hour 3

- Ask the r/SonnyAngel moderators for permission before posting.
- Ask Facebook and Discord group moderators for permission before posting.
- Submit the Product Hunt listing only if the maker account is eligible and the page is complete.

### Hour 5

- Publish the Seller Pro post and matching Story.
- Contact two seller-focused targets using the seller email—not the collector email.

### Hour 8

- Publish the phone-install Story sequence.
- Record clicks, sign-ups, checkout starts, paid subscriptions, and installation questions.

## First follow-up

- Send one follow-up after five business days only to recipients who did not decline.
- Never add cold contacts to a newsletter without consent.
- Stop contacting anyone who asks not to receive messages.

## Launch is not complete until

- At least one unaffiliated person completes sign-up and checkout successfully.
- iPhone and Android install instructions are confirmed on real devices.
- The privacy policy, terms, support contact, refund/cancellation explanation, and account-deletion process are publicly accessible.

# Seller Pro — complete launch feature sheet

## Positioning

Seller Pro is the business workspace inside Collector Vault for collectible sellers. It brings purchase orders, supplier details, seller-controlled Whatnot sales imports, operating expenses, inventory indicators, giveaway draws, and launch content into one dashboard.

## Purchase-order and expense tracking

Sellers can record:

- Figure cases, loose inventory, shipping supplies, and other purchases
- Saved supplier or manually entered vendor
- Purchase-order or invoice number
- Item description
- Case count, units per case, and total units
- Subtotal, shipping, tax, discount, and total cost
- Order and expected-arrival dates
- Order status
- Payment method, payment status, and payment date
- Carrier and tracking number
- Received date and internal notes

The order book keeps open purchases and received inventory visible beside sales activity.

## Supplier organizer

The supplier CRM stores:

- Business and contact names
- Email, phone, and website
- Typical lead time
- Minimum order amount
- Payment terms
- Supplier rating and private notes

It can be used for distributors, wholesalers, packaging vendors, and other inventory sources.

## Supported Whatnot workflow

Seller Pro does **not** request or store a seller’s Whatnot password. The seller exports their own CSV from Whatnot Seller Hub and uploads that file to Collector Vault.

The supported import can organize available fields such as:

- Order identifier and buyer handle
- Product and quantity
- Sale date and status
- Gross sales
- Commission and payment-processing fees
- Seller-paid shipping
- Payout or resulting earnings

Seller Pro uses the imported sales data with recorded business costs to show revenue, expenses, payouts, inventory indicators, and estimated profit. Figures are operating estimates and must be reconciled against Whatnot statements, supplier invoices, bank records, and tax records.

Approved phrase: **“Import your own Whatnot Seller Hub CSV for expense and sales tracking.”**

Do not say: **“Connects directly to your Whatnot account”** or **“automatically syncs Whatnot.”** Those features do not currently exist.

## Seller overview

- Gross-sales total
- Estimated net-profit snapshot
- Product-cost, platform-fee, and shipping summaries when data is available
- Open purchase orders
- Estimated units on hand
- Recent sales and purchasing activity ledger

## Giveaway wheel and audit history

Seller Pro includes an in-app wheel for lawful, free-entry promotional giveaways. Sellers can:

- Enter up to 100 unique entrants
- Disclose the giveaway name and exact prize
- Record eligibility and official rules
- Securely shuffle entries
- Select a winner using browser cryptographic randomness
- Optionally remove a winner after each draw
- Save a private draw history
- Save entrant count, winner, timestamp, and an entry-list integrity hash

The wheel requires certification that no purchase, payment, tip, bid, or subscription improves a person’s odds. It must not be used during or in connection with a Whatnot show; sellers should use Whatnot’s own giveaway tools there.

Do not promote paid raffles or “waffles.” If an audience uses those terms, say: **“Use the wheel only for free-entry giveaways where permitted; paid chance-based promotions are not supported.”**

## Exact product description

Collector Vault is an independent subscription workspace for collectors and collectible sellers. Members can browse and search the catalog; organize owned figures, quantities, duplicates, favorites, wishlists, ISOs, DISOs, and ready-to-trade items; review dated asking-price estimates and linked comparable listings when enough exact matches are available; receive source-following announcements; opt into reciprocal trade matching and private Trade Chat; and install the web app directly on an iPhone or Android home screen.

Seller Pro adds detailed purchase orders, supplier CRM, seller-controlled Whatnot Seller Hub CSV imports, sales and expense summaries, inventory indicators, estimated-profit reporting, a compliant free-entry giveaway-selection wheel with private audit history, and ready-to-copy launch content.

## Complete live feature inventory

### Collector organization

- Dashboard with collection-completion percentage and owned, wishlist, ISO, DISO, and trade totals.
- Searchable full catalog by figure name, alias, series, or rarity.
- Series filtering and full-directory browsing from ISO and DISO.
- Owned status, quantity, duplicate count, favorite, wishlist, ISO, DISO, and ready-to-trade status.
- Full-contain image presentation so artwork is not cropped.
- Image provenance link and verification date where available.
- Dated asking-price estimate, range, confidence, methodology, and comparable links when an exact defensible match is available.

### Trade Chat

- Member-controlled opt-in discoverability and public display name.
- Reciprocal matching: the other member has a figure you want and wants a figure you marked ready to trade.
- Private conversation tied to the two proposed figures.
- Live message updates, pending/active/closed status, block, and report controls.
- Safety reminders. Collector Vault does not authenticate users, inspect items, hold funds, insure shipments, or guarantee trades.

### Alerts and SMS

- Restock, drop, launch, and inventory announcement feed.
- Filter by announcement type and open the original product/source link.
- Personal store-and-website watchlist.
- Optional SMS verification, event-type preferences, enable/disable control, timezone, and quiet hours.
- Adding a store URL does not create automatic monitoring by itself; it matches that URL against Collector Vault's verified alert feed.

### Membership and phone installation

- Email account sign-up and sign-in.
- Stripe-hosted monthly or yearly checkout and membership gate.
- Install button and iPhone/Android home-screen instructions.
- Progressive web app opens in its own standalone window; no App Store or Google Play account is required.

### Seller Pro

- Seller overview: gross sales, estimated net profit, open POs, units-on-hand indicator, revenue, product cost, fees plus shipping, and recent business activity.
- Detailed order entry: saved supplier or vendor, PO/invoice number, order type, item description, cases, units per case, total units, subtotal, freight, tax/duties, discount, final total, payment method/status/date, order status/date, ETA, received date, carrier, tracking, and notes.
- Supplier CRM: business and contact, email, phone, website, lead time, minimum order, payment terms, rating, and notes.
- Seller-controlled Whatnot CSV workflow: the seller exports their own Seller Hub ledger/order file and uploads it. Seller Pro maps available order ID, buyer, product, date, quantity, gross sales, commission, processing fee, seller-paid shipping, payout, and status fields.
- No Whatnot password is requested or stored. There is no automatic Whatnot account sync.
- Free-entry giveaway wheel: up to 100 unique entrants, disclosed name/prize/eligibility/rules, cryptographic shuffle and selection, optional winner removal, entry hash, timestamp, winner, and private draw history.
- The wheel is not for paid raffles or waffles. No purchase, payment, tip, bid, or subscription may improve odds. Do not use it during or in connection with a Whatnot show.
- Launch Studio with copyable captions, Reel structure, rollout schedule, and seller campaign prompts.

## Outreach rules

- Personalize the first two sentences for every recipient.
- Contact only publicly listed business or creator addresses.
- Ask moderators before posting in groups.
- Do not scrape or purchase personal email lists.
- Do not send more than one follow-up.
- Do not offer payment without a written deliverable and disclosure requirement.
- Do not call a store, creator or platform a partner without written agreement.
- Do not market paid raffles or waffles; describe the wheel only as a free, compliant giveaway-selection tool.
- Do not use Whatnot shows or chat to redirect buyers to off-platform purchases.
